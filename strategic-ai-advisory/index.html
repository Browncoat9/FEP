🧭 Strategic AI Advisory

Helping organizations evaluate AI readiness, select the right tools, and build a roadmap for adoption.

Overview

Many organizations know they want to use AI but don't know where to start. This engagement framework helps teams assess their current processes, identify high-ROI automation opportunities, evaluate available tools, and produce a prioritized AI adoption roadmap — without the hype.

Approach

Process Mapping ROI Modeling Vendor Evaluation Change Management

Deliverables

A structured 4-week advisory engagement covering current-state assessment, opportunity identification, tool selection, and a 90-day implementation roadmap with clear ownership and milestones.
